Who Am I?

Hey, I’m Dark Reycon. I like messing around with code, breaking things to see how they work, and then putting them back together (hopefully better). Most of the time I’m building little projects to automate stuff or experimenting with how websites and security work. I’m still learning, but that’s the fun part.

I’m a huge anime and manga fan! When I’m not coding or building projects, you’ll usually find me watching my favorite shows or reading the latest manga. Boruto has been one of my top favorites lately—it inspires me to keep pushing forward and learning new things, just like the characters do.

What I Want to Learn

I’m always curious and love learning new things. Here’s what’s on my list next:

  • PHP, SQL, and MySQL – to build better web apps
  • SQL injections – understanding common security flaws
  • HTTPS servers – how secure websites really work
  • Linux terminal – mastering the command line
  • Nmap & Hydra – exploring network security tools

Why I Do This

I’m an INTP, so I naturally love thinking, analyzing, and figuring things out. Programming and security let me challenge my mind and solve problems in creative ways. I’ve read that hacking is one of the most common and rewarding paths for curious programmers, and it really fits my style—I like working independently, as a freelancer, with no strict rules or schedules.
For me, coding isn’t just work; it’s a way to explore, experiment, and push my limits.
